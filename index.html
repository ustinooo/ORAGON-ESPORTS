Team Composition and Roles

Ensure that each player is skilled in their respective roles. For example, in games like League of Legends, you need players specializing in specific positions like top lane, mid lane, ADC, support, and jungle. Chemistry and Communication: The ability to communicate effectively and work well together is crucial. Team chemistry can often be the difference between victory and defeat.

Practice Training and Development

Establish a rigorous and consistent practice schedule. This includes individual skill improvement, team strategies, and scrimmages against other teams. Coaching and Analysis: Invest in a good coach and possibly an analyst. A coach can help with strategy, while an analyst can break down gameplay to find areas of improvement.

Branding and Marketing

Create a strong team identity, including a memorable name, logo, and team colors. This helps in building a fan base and attracting sponsors. Social Media Presence: Maintain an active social media presence on platforms like Instagram, YouTube, and Facebook. Engaging content, regular updates, and interaction with fans are key to growing your audience.

GOALS

OUR MAIN GOALS

OBJECTIVE:

Utilize the team's platform and resources to positively impact the

Filipino community through charitable activities and donations.

PLAYER - FOCUSED GOALS:

Commit to 2-3 hours of daily practice, use analytics for skill tracking, and allocate weekly gameplay review time. Participate in three scrimmages weekly, refine communication, and clearly define roles. Follow a fitness routine, hold weekly mental health sessions, and promote a healthy lifestyle.

TEAM - FOCUSED GOALS:

Participate in monthly local tournaments, analyze performance post-tournament, and focus on improvement areas. Maintain a strong social media presence, establish a streaming schedule, and launch team merchandise.
